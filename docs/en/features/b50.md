# Score System

The Best 50 (b50) and Best 100 (b100) charts are JiETNG's flagship features, displaying your highest-rated scores in beautifully designed visualizations.

## What is Best 50?

The "Best 50" system is Maimai DX's official ranking method, consisting of:

- **Best 35**: Your top 35 scores from **old version songs (æ—§æ›² - songs from previous versions)**
- **Best 15**: Your top 15 scores from **current version songs (æ–°æ›² - songs from the current version)**

Your **Maimai DX Rating** is the sum of these 50 scores.

## Commands

### Basic Best Charts

```
b50          # Generate Best 50 chart
b100         # Generate Best 100 chart
best50       # Alternative for b50
best100      # Alternative for b100
```

### Variations

```

ab35         # All Best 35
apb50        # All Perfect Best 50 (only AP/AP+ scores)
idlb50       # Ideal Best 50 (simulated SSS+ scores)
```

## Advanced Usage

### Filtering Scores

You can apply filters to customize your b50 output:

#### Filter by Level

```
b50 -lv 15              # Only level 15 songs
b50 -lv 14 15           # Levels 14-15
b50 -lv 13+             # Level 13.7+ and above
```

#### Filter by Rating

```
b50 -ra 200             # Rating 200+ only
b50 -ra 180 200         # Rating 180-200
```

#### Filter by Achievement

```
b50 -scr 100.5          # 100.5%+ achievement
b50 -scr 99 100         # 99%-100% achievement
```

#### Filter by DX Score

```
b50 -dx 95              # DX score 95%+
b50 -dx 90 95           # DX score 90-95%
```

### Combining Filters

You can combine multiple filters:

```
b50 -lv 15 -scr 100.5            # Level 15 songs with 100.5%+
b50 -ra 200 -dx 95               # Rating 200+ with DX score 95%+
b50 -lv 14 15 -scr 99.5 -dx 90   # Complex filter
```

## Chart Types Explained

### Best 50 (b50)

Standard ranking chart following official rules:
- Top 35 from old version songs
- Top 15 from current version songs
- Your actual Maimai DX rating

**Use case**: Check your official rating and progress

### Best 100 (b100)

Extended version showing more scores:
- Top 70 from old version songs
- Top 30 from current version songs
- See beyond your rating songs

**Use case**: Find songs just below your b50 threshold

### All Best 50 (ab50)

Ignores song version distinction:
- Top 50 scores regardless of version
- May be all old version or all current version songs
- Shows your absolute best plays

**Use case**: See your highest achievements regardless of category

### All Perfect Best 50 (apb50)

Shows only perfect plays:
- Only AP (All Perfect) and AP+ scores
- Ranked by rating
- Track your perfect combos

**Use case**: Monitor your perfect play progress

### Ideal Best 50 (idlb50)

Theoretical maximum rating:
- Simulates SSS+ (100.5%+) on all songs
- Shows potential rating gain
- Motivates improvement

**Use case**: Set goals for rating improvement

## Understanding the Output

### Rating Calculation

Each song's rating is calculated as:

Achievement Modifier = Based on your achievement %

Final Rating = Base Rating + Achievement Modifier

Achievement modifiers:
- **100.5000%+**: +2.0 rating (SSS+)

## Common Questions

### Why is my rating different from in-game?

- JiETNG updates only when you run `maimai update`
- Official rating updates after each play
- Sync your scores regularly to stay current

### Some scores are missing?

Make sure you've played the songs recently. Older scores may not appear if:
- They've been replaced by better scores
- The song was removed from the game
- Data sync issues (try `maimai update` again)

### Can I see historical b50?

Currently, JiETNG shows your current b50. Historical tracking is planned for future updates.
